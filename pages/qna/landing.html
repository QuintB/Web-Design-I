Welcome to Q&A
We're two guys that are just as passionate about great products and technology as you are. We love finding new things, putting them to the test, and letting you guys know how it turns out. We enjoy teaching the community with free tutorials. We like to get together and talk about last week's tech news. This is our home.

We Talk.
Q&A got started as a podcast. Every week, Adam and I would look out for tech news that interested us, and include that in our roundup for the week. Turns out, people like listening to two friends talk about things they’re passaionate about! We cover any and all news we can find, and always look forward to the submissions we get from our listeners on Facebook and Twitter.

Q&A with Q&A is available on iTunes Podcasts, YouTube, SoundCloud, and of course, our own website here. Take a listen at the latest Q&A by clicking the play button to the right!
